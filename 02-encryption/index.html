Stardate 46257.6

While enroute to Rigel IV for routine maintenance, we have received what appears to be a distress signal from an unknown vessel. Curiously, our long-range scanners cannot detect any ships at the beacon's source. We will know more after we have fed the signal into the Universal Translator.
